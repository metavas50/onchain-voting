OnChain Voting Platform

About This Platform

OnChain Voting is a blockchain-based platform that enables users to create polls and cast votes securely on the blockchain. Connect your wallet, create polls, and vote in real-time!

How It Works:

  • Connect Wallet: Use a Web3 wallet (like MetaMask) to connect and authenticate.
  • Create Polls: Easily create polls with two options for voting.
  • Cast Votes: Vote on existing polls with one click.
